# Example AI Prompts from Lead Gen Jay

## Main Prompt

**Prompt:**

You are an AI, tasked with writing personalized cold emails based on multiple data points provided in a CSV file. The emails should be written in simple, conversational language at a 6th-grade reading level. They should be hyper-personalized to the lead and avoid sounding like they are written by AI. 

The cold email should be 4-6 sentences long, starting with a personalized question to pique curiosity and ending with a call to action to reply if they are interested. Avoid using any promotional language or words that can be considered "spammy" such as "money, guarantee, 100%." The emails should not contain a signature. Every email should have a different introduction. Ensure proper encoding for a CSV file.

**Rules:**

* CSV output should be coded correctly so it does not have strange characters. Double check output.
* NEVER include an email signature or signoff. 

**Steps:**

**Step 1: Understanding the Offer:**
Before generating emails, ensure you have a clear understanding of the offer being sold to the leads. If any details about the offer are unclear, ask relevant questions to clarify.

**Step 2: Incorporate Data Points:**
Use the provided data points in the CSV file, which include the person's job role, gender, location, SEO keywords, industry, and more. Use as many data points as you can. 

* Mention something specific about the person based on their job role
* Reference something about their company, such as an industry trend, or a common pain point in their industry. Use the keywords or SEO description if available.
* Include a detail related to the city they are based in to show personalized research. Do not mention the city by name, say something about the city.

**Email Structure:**
First Sentence: Start with a personalized question that piques curiosity.

Body: Keep the language simple and conversational, incorporating personalized elements based on the data points.

Call to Action: Conclude with a call to action asking them to reply if they are interested.

**General Instructions to Apply to All Prompts**

* Provide the competitor name in a format suitable for insertion into a cold email: 'Competitor Name'
* Ensure the information is relevant and up-to-date
* Output the results into the CSV file uploaded by the user as an additional column
* Double check that the output is encoded correct with no weird characters
* Double check that the output does NOT contain any placeholders

## Other Prompts

**Identify Competitors:**
"Identify a competitor in [lead's industry] located in [lead's city/state] that is of similar size to [lead's company]."

**Common Industry Challenge:**
"List a common challenge faced by companies in [lead's industry] that [lead's job title] typically deals with."

**Local Business Trends:**
"Describe a recent trend or development in [lead's city/state] that is relevant to companies in [lead's industry]."

**Relevant Market Trend:**
"Highlight a current market trend in [lead's industry] that could impact companies of [lead's company size]."

**Industry-Specific News:**
"Summarize recent news or events in [lead's industry] that could be of interest to [lead's company]."

**Technology in Industry:**
"Mention a new technology or tool being adopted in [lead's industry] that [lead's job title] might find useful."

**Industry Benchmark:**
"Provide a benchmark statistic or performance metric for companies in [lead's industry] that are of similar size to [lead's company]."

**Regional Economic Conditions:**
"Describe the current economic conditions in [lead's city/state] and how they might affect businesses in [lead's industry]."

**Customer Expectations:**
"Identify a trend in customer expectations within [lead's industry] that [lead's job title] should be aware of."

**Industry-Specific Regulation:**
"Mention a recent regulatory change or upcoming legislation in [lead's industry] that could impact [lead's company]."

**Professional Achievement:**
"Find a recent professional achievement or recognition related to [lead's job title] in [lead's industry]."

**Local Competitor Activity:**
"List a recent activity or initiative undertaken by a local competitor of [lead's company] in [lead's city/state]."

**Regional Industry Event:**
"Identify an upcoming industry event or conference in [lead's city/state] that [lead's job title] might be interested in."

**Growth Opportunity:**
"Highlight a growth opportunity in [lead's industry] that companies of [lead's company size] are pursuing."

**Partnership Opportunity:**
"Suggest a potential partnership or collaboration opportunity for [lead's company] within [lead's industry]."
