### 2.6 QUICK PERSONALIZATION DECISION TREE

**Use this to decide which personalization to include in Email 1** (10-15 min total research per prospect):

**START**: Found recent company news (funding, expansion, acquisition, hiring, award)?
- **YES** → Use news-based personalization in Email 1
- **NO** → Go to next question

**Found recent LinkedIn/social post from decision-maker** (last 30-60 days)?
- **YES** → Reference their post in Email 1
- **NO** → Go to next question

**Identified 1-2 similar competitors** (same market, same size)?
- **YES** → Use competitor name-drop or social proof in Email 1
- **NO** → Use vertical-level insights from Layer 3 (no individual personalization needed)

**Recommended Approach**:
- **High-value prospects** (enterprise, strategic accounts): Spend 15-20 min on Layer 2 research, find personalization hook
- **Mid-value prospects**: Spend 10 min, use personalization if found quickly
- **High-volume prospects**: Spend 5 min, skip if no obvious hook, use vertical template

**Goal**: 30-50% of emails include Layer 2 personalization (individual company/executive), 50-70% use vertical template only
